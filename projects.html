Multi-Model Image Classifier — Columbia SHAPE Program

Flask web app with three computer vision models: a stock ResNet-50 pretrained on ImageNet (1,000 categories), a fine-tuned dog breed classifier (37 breeds), and a fine-tuned food classifier (101 categories). Upload a photo or point your webcam — it classifies in real time and shows top-5 predictions with confidence bars. The custom models were trained from scratch using transfer learning on top of ResNet-50.

Poker Hand Probability Calculator — ModSim Class

Team project (with Raj Awasthi and Rowan Sponberg). Enter each player's hole cards and the current river, and the program runs thousands of Monte Carlo simulations — randomly dealing the remaining cards and evaluating who wins each time — to output accurate win percentages for every active player.

Nash Equilibrium Simulator — ModSim Class

Simulates repeated game play between bots to observe Nash Equilibria emerging through reinforcement learning. Runs a 10-player round-robin tournament across classic game theory scenarios — Prisoner's Dilemma, Stag Hunt, Battle of the Sexes, and Rock Paper Scissors — tracking how player strategies evolve over time.
